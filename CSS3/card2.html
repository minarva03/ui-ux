ABOUT

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg (born May 14, 1984) is an American media magnate,internet entrepreneur,and philanthropist. He is known for co-founding Facebook, Inc. and serves as its chairman, chief executive officer, and controlling shareholder. He also is a co-founder of the solar sail spacecraft development project Breakthrough Starshot & serves as one of its board.
